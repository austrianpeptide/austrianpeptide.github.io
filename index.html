AtPS

Information

1st Announcement of 10th Austrian Peptide Symposium (AtPS) taking place on December 2nd 2021 in the Van-Swieten Hall in Vienna.

The following speakers already agreed to join the meeting, which will be held as a one-day live event (if Corona rules in Austria will allow this):

Dagmar Zweytick – University of Graz, Austria
Ashraf Brik – Technion, Israel
Nina Hartramopf, University of Zurich, Switzerland

Registration will open in August.

The 10th AtPS will also see the first Bachem Award presentation and the call is now open for early career scientists (postdocs or junior group leaders) who have made outstanding contributions to the chemistry and/or biology of peptides in the three years preceding the date of selection. For details, please download the call PDF. Submission deadline: September 15th 2021.

The symposium might switch to a hybrid or online event depending on the Corona situation.
