Dear Colleagues, Friends and Members of the Peptide Community,

Due to the SARS-Cov2 pandemic and its recent developments we have finally decided to postpone the 10th Austrian Peptide Symposium to December 2021.

We’re disappointed not to be able to celebrate the 10th anniversary of our symposium with you this year but we also believe that we need to consider your and our safety first.

As this symposium will constitute the 10th anniversary of the AtPS, we now plan for a very exciting meeting between November 30th and December 2nd 2021 in combination with the 5th International Conference for Circular Proteins and Peptides.

In 2021, we will also present, for the first time, the Bachem Award for “Biology & Chemistry of Peptides” to a young colleague working in Austria, Switzerland or Germany. Details will follow on the AtPS website.

We’re looking forward to seeing you in Vienna next year at the Van-Swieten Symposium Hall.

As always there will be ample opportunities for oral and poster presentations (based on submitted abstracts).
