Bachem Award
Dear participants, speakers and supporters of the Austrian Peptide Symposium,
Since the Austrian government just imposed a general lock down as of Monday next week (November 22nd 2021), we cannot have the Peptide Symposium as live event in Vienna.
Instead we will switch to a full online format!
We will do this by keeping the already announced program (dinner and networking session will have to be cancelled), which we as chairs will present live from a studio in Vienna supported by an experience technical crew. All presentations will be streamed online and poster contributions will be made available as pre-recorder 3 min presentations. (To this end, we will send out soon detailed information to all poster presenter on how to prepare and upload this video.)
We understand that this is very different from a live meeting and that you will miss out on face-to-face networking and discussions.
Still we hope you all can and will participate. Registration fees will cover the costs involved with streaming of the event.
Again we apologize for any inconvenience, and we deeply regret that switching to an online symposium format cannot be avoided.
Please let us know if we can be of any assistance.
Kind regards,
Christian Gruber & Christian Becker
AtPS Chairs
P.S.: We anticipate to send out detailed information about online access to all registered participants, as well as further information for speakers and presenters within the next week. There will be a practice dial in for speakers before the event.

Bachem Award
The 10th AtPS will also see the first Bachem Award presentation for early career scientists (postdocs or junior group leaders) who have made outstanding contributions to the chemistry and/or biology of peptides in the three years preceding the date of selection. For details, please download the announcement PDF. See this years winners.
Topics include:
  • Peptide Chemistry and Synthesis
  • Peptide Drug Development and Delivery
  • Peptide Structural Biology
  • Peptide Pharmacology
